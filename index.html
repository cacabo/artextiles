    Welcome to AR Textiles

    Full process, high-tech textile manufacturing.

    Quality production to meet market demand

    We operate one of the most modern mills in the world. The plant is located in Robersonville NC, USA. Our proximity to both cotton fields and the port of Norfolk enables us to supply yarn throughout the world.

    Our customers benefit from automated processes with minimal manual intervention. Our automated packaging system, measured length packages, and use of Digi winding make our products especially attractive to the market.

    About our plant

    Our plant has a capacity to produce 2.6 million lbs (12,000 tonnes) of high quality yarn per annum, with a count range NE 6-30/1. All yarn is made with state-of-the-art technology from leading textile machinery manufactures like Schlafhorst, Trutzschler, Electro-jet, and Pneumafil. The complete textile laboratory is from Uster Switzerland.

    Our contamination free yarn is made with US grown cotton lab-tested for staple length, micronaire, tenacity, trash content, and purity. Opening using Trutzschler machines and spinning using the Saurer ACO 8 ensure the highest quality.
